B U G A T T I   V E Y R O N



The Bugatti Veyron is an iconic supercar produced by the French luxury automotive manufacturer Bugatti. Introduced in 2005, the Veyron quickly gained worldwide recognition for its extraordinary performance, cutting-edge technology, and exquisite design. It was a groundbreaking achievement in the automotive industry and pushed the limits of what was thought to be possible in a road-legal production car.


Named after French racing driver Pierre Veyron, who won the 24 Hours of Le Mans in 1939 for Bugatti, the Veyron was designed to be the ultimate expression of automotive engineering and luxury. It combined a striking exterior design with unparalleled power and speed, making it one of the most coveted and exclusive cars ever created.


The Bugatti Veyron's most remarkable feature is its staggering performance. Underneath its sleek and aerodynamic body, the Veyron housed an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine. This powerhouse produced an astonishing 1,001 horsepower in its early versions and later evolved into even more powerful variants. The car's immense power was channeled through a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system, enabling it to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in less than three seconds and reach a top speed of over 250 mph (400 km/h).


To achieve such exceptional performance, Bugatti incorporated cutting-edge technologies into the Veyron. The car featured advanced aerodynamics, carbon fiber construction, and a highly advanced suspension system to ensure stability and control at high speeds. It also boasted a host of innovative features, including a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, advanced braking system, and a complex cooling system to manage the engine's heat output.


Not only was the Veyron a technical marvel, but it also showcased unparalleled luxury and craftsmanship. The interior was meticulously designed, offering a blend of comfort and sophistication. High-quality materials, handcrafted finishes, and advanced infotainment and driver-assistance systems adorned the cabin, providing an exceptional driving experience for both the driver and passengers.


With its limited production numbers and astronomical price tag, the Bugatti Veyron became a symbol of exclusivity and prestige. It captured the imagination of automotive enthusiasts and collectors worldwide, cementing its status as a true automotive legend.


The Bugatti Veyron redefined what was possible in a road-legal car, combining breathtaking performance, cutting-edge technology, and exquisite craftsmanship. It remains an icon of automotive engineering and a testament to Bugatti's unwavering pursuit of automotive perfection.

PERFECT TECHNOLOGY WITH A UNIQUE DESIGN


The guiding principle of the genius car designer Ettore Bugatti, who came from a family of artists, was to always encase perfect technology in an aesthetically appealing exterior. This commitment lives on today: the designers gave the Veyron 16.4 Super Sport an utterly unique appearance – and at the same time, every single exterior modification serves to improve performance.  

 

 

 

 

Technical maxims 


Specialist expertise amassed over the course of almost ten years’ cutting-edge development enabled the Bugatti engineers to create an upgraded Veyron that opens up new dimensions of driving.  

All the modifications serve to create a more powerful vehicle with even more agile handling. The increased performance of the 16-cylinder engine is primarily due to the use of four larger turbochargers and intercoolers.

 

 

Chassis 


The high-performance sports car’s chassis was meticulously reworked to enable the unique driving power to be safely controlled. The main spring rates were slightly increased and the stabilizers were strengthened. In conjunction with the newly developed dampers, adapted from motorsport models, this enables perceptibly more precise control of the wheels and the vehicle. In addition to the high lateral potential of up to 1.4 G, the optimized interaction between the tyres and the smart four-wheel drive system enables perfect handling. A powerful torque of 1,500 Nm enables even higher acceleration values when coming out of a turn. All components of the drivetrain were extensively reinforced in order to safely transmit the immense forces. 

 

 

Aerodynamics 


The fine-tuned optimization of the bodyshell aerodynamics improves efficiency and ensures perfect balance in all driving scenarios. Modified air vents improve the airflow and cooling efficiency. The full-carbon monocoque features a new fiber structure that guarantees even higher torsional rigidity than the basic model with maximum passive safety and reduced weight.
