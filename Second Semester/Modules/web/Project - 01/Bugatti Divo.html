B U G A T T I    D I V O



The Bugatti Divo is an exceptional hypercar produced by the renowned French automaker Bugatti. Unveiled in 2018, the Divo stands as a limited-edition version of the Bugatti Chiron, with a specific focus on delivering enhanced agility, handling, and aerodynamics for track performance.

At the heart of the Bugatti Divo lies a mighty 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, akin to the powertrain found in the Chiron. This remarkable engine produces an astonishing 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque, catapulting the Divo from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.4 seconds. The top speed of the Divo is electronically limited to 236 mph, ensuring an exhilarating driving experience.

The design of the Bugatti Divo embodies a captivating and aggressive aesthetic that distinguishes it from the Chiron. The front end of the car boasts a broader grille, larger air intakes, reshaped headlights, and an imposing splitter, all contributing to improved aerodynamics. The rear of the Divo is characterized by a substantial fixed rear wing, a unique light strip, and a redesigned diffuser, which work together to optimize downforce and stability.

Aerodynamics played a pivotal role in the development of the Bugatti Divo. Through extensive wind tunnel testing and meticulous engineering, the Divo underwent several aerodynamic modifications. These include a revised front fascia, enhanced airflow management, new NACA ducts, and a sophisticated rear spoiler. These enhancements generate increased downforce, enabling the Divo to achieve exceptional cornering abilities and enhanced stability at high speeds.

One of the hallmarks of the Bugatti Divo is its exclusivity. With only 40 units produced, the Divo is an extremely rare and highly coveted hypercar. Each vehicle is meticulously handcrafted, allowing customers to personalize their cars to a remarkable degree. The limited production run, combined with the exceptional performance and bespoke nature, makes the Divo a truly exceptional and sought-after collector's car.

The Bugatti Divo comes with a significant price tag, surpassing that of the Bugatti Chiron. With a base price of around €5 million (approximately $5.8 million), the Divo represents the epitome of luxury and performance. However, the final price of a Divo can vary significantly depending on the chosen customization options, allowing buyers to create a truly unique and personalized masterpiece.

The Divo's limited production and exclusivity make it a symbol of prestige and automotive excellence. Its remarkable performance, combined with the meticulous attention to detail in design and engineering, establishes the Divo as a true marvel of automotive engineering.

Owning a Bugatti Divo grants access to an elite community of automotive enthusiasts who appreciate the finest craftsmanship, unparalleled performance, and timeless beauty. With its combination of power, rarity, and bespoke luxury, the Bugatti Divo stands as a remarkable testament to Bugatti's unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of speed and engineering prowess.

 IMPOSING BEAUTY


 An entirely new architecture was developed for the front of the DIVO. The lightweight LED headlights are ultra-compact, just 35mm tall. Their vertical positioning allows for additional air intakes to cool the brakes. Daytime running lights on the outer edge of the car give the DIVO a sense of greater width and strength.







 

 

ENHANCED AERODYNAMICS 



An homage to the iconic BUGATTI Type 57SC Atlantic, the DIVO’s central fin also plays a functional role, preventing air from causing turbulence at the rear. The NACA air intake on the roof creates an optical connection with the air outlet in the bonnet and helps guide air towards the non-retractable rear wing. Measuring 183cm wide, the hydraulically actuated rear wing is 23% wider than the CHIRON. This larger surface increases the performance of the air brake. 




 

 

 

INTENSE ATTRACTION


The rear of the DIVO is composed for maximum impact. Partly 3D-printed, the rear grill is characterised by a stunning tail light system made from lightweight fins of varying sizes. At the outer edge, the fins become wider, creating a more intense light and emphasizing the DIVO’s powerfully wide body. 








 

 

CENTER STAGE. THE DRIVER 


Inside, DIVO Racing Blue provides a stark optical split, ensuring the asymmetric color layout focuses on the driver. The newly designed seats offer greater lateral support with the lightweight central console giving greater comfort thanks to larger armrests and calf supports. Alcantara partly covers the steering wheel, which also has larger shift paddles on each side.
