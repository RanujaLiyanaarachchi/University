B U G A T T I    L A    V O I T U R E    N O I R E



The Bugatti La Voiture Noire is a luxury sports car produced by the French automotive manufacturer Bugatti. It is an exclusive and highly limited edition model, with only one unit ever produced. "La Voiture Noire" translates to "The Black Car" in English, and the name pays homage to the iconic Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic, a classic car known for its black exterior.


The design of the Bugatti La Voiture Noire is sleek, aerodynamic, and embodies elegance and power. Its body is predominantly black, emphasizing its unique and distinct presence. The car features a curvaceous and flowing silhouette, with smooth lines that seamlessly blend together. The exterior is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, showcasing the exquisite craftsmanship that Bugatti is renowned for.


Underneath its stunning exterior, the La Voiture Noire boasts impressive performance capabilities. It is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, delivering a staggering 1,479 horsepower. This immense power enables the car to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 2.4 seconds, with a top speed of approximately 261 mph (420 km/h). The Bugatti La Voiture Noire combines luxury, artistry, and extraordinary engineering to create a truly exceptional driving experience.


The interior of the Bugatti La Voiture Noire is a masterclass in opulence and refinement. It features premium materials, exquisite leather upholstery, and meticulously handcrafted details. The cockpit is designed to provide utmost comfort and convenience, with state-of-the-art technology seamlessly integrated. The interior is a harmonious blend of modern innovation and timeless elegance, offering a luxurious environment for the driver and passengers.


Owning the Bugatti La Voiture Noire is a privilege reserved for a select few. Its unparalleled exclusivity, breathtaking design, and extraordinary performance make it a true masterpiece of automotive engineering. The car represents the pinnacle of automotive luxury and serves as a symbol of status and sophistication.


The Bugatti La Voiture Noire is not only a remarkable automobile in terms of its performance and design, but it also carries a significant historical and cultural significance. As a tribute to the Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic, the La Voiture Noire embodies the spirit of the original car, which was renowned for its elegance and rarity.


The Type 57SC Atlantic, produced in the 1930s, is considered one of the most beautiful and iconic cars ever made. Its streamlined shape, elongated hood, and distinctive dorsal fin made it instantly recognizable. Today, only four Atlantic models exist, with one famously owned by designer Ralph Lauren. The Bugatti La Voiture Noire pays homage to this timeless classic by capturing its essence and reinterpreting it for the modern era.


Beyond its physical attributes, the Bugatti La Voiture Noire represents the epitome of luxury and exclusivity. With only one unit ever produced, it has become a highly sought-after collector's item. The car was unveiled at the Geneva International Motor Show in 2019 and was quickly sold to an undisclosed buyer for a record-breaking price, making it the most expensive new car ever sold.


The Bugatti brand, known for its commitment to craftsmanship and engineering excellence, has created a masterpiece in the form of the La Voiture Noire. It stands as a symbol of automotive innovation, pushing the boundaries of what is possible in terms of performance and design.


The Bugatti La Voiture Noire is a true automotive masterpiece, representing the intersection of art, luxury, and engineering prowess. It captures the imagination of car enthusiasts and collectors around the world, cementing its place in automotive history as an extraordinary creation that will continue to inspire generations to come.

FLOWING LINES 


The fin running down the center of “La Voiture Noire” echoes the Atlantic’s legendary dorsal seam. It is a unique symbiosis of history and modernity, which subtly runs into the flowing lines of the perforated rear. 

 

 

 

 

DOWN TO THE SMALLEST DETAILS 


Each component has been crafted by hand, accentuating the level of BUGATTI’s manufacturing quality. The carbon fiber body has a deep black gloss, interrupted only by the ultrafine fiber structure. Perfectly harmonized materials merge with the finish to leave the surface gleaming vividly.  

 

 

 

 

UNCOMPROMISING PERFECTION 


The wheels exemplify the car’s potent appearance. A special program was used to calculate the optimal material thickness for the alloy wheels during construction. 

 

 

 

 

THE HEART OF LA VOITURE NOIRE 


Centerpiece of “La Voiture Noire” is its beating heart, the iconic 16-cylinder engine. This technical masterpiece is unique in the automobile world and a true mechanical highlight. The 16-cylinder, 8.0-liter engine delivers 1,103 kW/1,500 PS and 1,600 Nm of torque.
